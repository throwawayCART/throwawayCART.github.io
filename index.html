We Are CART Robotics!

We are the official team that represents CART in VEX Robotics tournaments. We are a team exclusive to students from Fresno and Clovis school districts that enroll into the Robotics Lab at the Center for Advanced Research and Technology.

About Us

As the CART Robotics team, we strive to achieve excellence as we compete against other teams in VEX Robotics tournaments. Our goal is to create a friendly team environment where groups work together to achieve large tasks. Each year we will be tasked with creating an original, functioning robot that can accomplish tasks in the most productive way.
